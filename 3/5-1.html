1st Generation

The period of first generation was from 1945 to 1953 [2].Computers of first generation used Vacuum Tubes as the basic components for circuitry and Magnetic Drums for memory. Machine language was used as programming language.Computers of this generation were depended on machine language to perform operations and could be able to solve one problem at a time. Example for the machines of this era were the UNIVAC and ENIAC machines. These vacuum tubes produced a lot of heat and the installations used to fuse frequently. Therefore, they were very expensive and only large organizations were able to afford it.
