2nd Generation

The period of second generation was from 1954 to 1965.In second generation computers were replaced with Transistors. Magnetic cores were used as the primary memory and magnetic tape, magnetic disks were used as secondary storage devices. High level languages were used as programming language.This generation computers were cheaper, consumed less power, more compact in size, more reliable and faster than the first generation machines made of vacuum tubes. IBM 1620 and IBM 7094 were in this generation.
